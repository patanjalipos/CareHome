Connecting and communicating with me - When you are spending time with someone, what helps you to have a good chat or connection?

Include tips and tricks sicha s tone of voice, speed we are talking, how we stand or sit beside the peson, how we explain what we are talking about the use of other tools such as communication boards/cards etc

Are there any physical gestures or signs, in your face or body, you use to communicate how you are feeling to others

Include lack of eye contact, looking aware, screwing up face or blushing if the person isn't understanding what is being said, turning their good ear to you when they are having difficulty in hearing you etc

Connecting with Humour - what kinds of things do you find funny and enjoy

Gain the information from the person, their loved ones or previous assessments. Include whether it is particular programs such as carry on films/still game/you've been framed for example, dark sense of humour, silly things like tickling or acting the clown

Are there types of physical affection you like and don't like?

Include kiss on the cheek or hand, hugs, arm around your shoulder, holding hands, playing with your hair, a shoulder rub, or consider if there is anything they would like us to know about how they feel about physical touch

Are there previous or current close relationships with a partner that you would like us to know about?

Allow the person to express anything they wish around personal relationship they would like us to know and incorporate into their support plans

is there anything else you would like us to know that is important for you in terms of your needs around intimacy

It is really important that this question is asked sensitively and if the person is unable to tell us that we speak to their significant others to gain insight to promote their intimacy needs and wishes.
