Background - What is the background knowledge of the person?

How long have you known them, how long have they been in the care home, what is their ACP - preferred place of care is?

RESTORE 2 - SOFT SIGNS OF DETERIORATION IN HEALTH

What are the soft signs that the resident displays when they are brewing an infection or becoming unwell

RESTORE 2 - NORMAL BASELINE OBSERVATIONS

If the resident has COPD for exmaple what is their normal oxygen saturation or presentation - what is the residents baseline.

Assessment - What are the symptoms and change you see in front of you?

Include the question in your response and consider the following: what has changed for them in the last 24hours? include behaviour, mobility, pain, food and fluid intake and output,

Assessment - What are their current clinical observations?

Include: Date and time of ob's being taken, TEMP, BP, PULSE, OXYGEN SATURATION, RESPIRATIONS, BLOOD GLUCOSE and overall NEWS score

Background - Only complete for OOH calls - Does the resident have a Key information summary (KIS), does it state anything relevant to this situation in the special notes?

Detail any ACP or ReSPECT documentation which is currently in place with regards to escalation to hospital, interventions etc

Background - When was the person last reviewed by a medical practitioner and why?

Please put as much detail as possible into this part about GP involvement or previous OOH input in relation to the recent care of this person

Background - Include summary of any relevant past medical history (including for example communication needs/cognitive impairment/allergies)

Please include the question in the response - include any stress and distress in new surroundings or people.

Include statement the resident currently takes the following medications..... name of medication, dose, frequency, any covert pathways/how the medication is administered in place or any recent omissions/errors etc

Please include name and contact details of POA/Guardian in the other box and any details that are relevant within treatment plan and their awareness of the situation
